Hi, I’m a PhD student at São Paulo State University (UNESP) where I work in the intersection between Visualization and Machine Learning.

I am interested in a variety of topics of Visualization, Visual Analytics and Machine Learning. Currently, I am working on scalable visualizations to ease the inspection of dimensionality reduction result, as well as creating visual metaphors and techniques for single-cell RNA sequencing data.
