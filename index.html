Biography

Wilson Marcílio-Jr is a Ph.D. candidate in Explainable AI at São Paulo State University. He works with visualization for interpretation and easy exploration of multidimensional data. His research focuses on proposing novel techniques for dimensionality reduction and explainability of machine learning models.
