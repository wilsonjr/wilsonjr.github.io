Wilson Marcílio-Jr

Hi, I am a Ph.D. student at São Paulo State University. I work with visualization for interpretation and easy exploration of multidimensional data. In my Ph.D. studies, I am focusing on the visual exploration of dimensionality reduction and other machine learning models.

I received a BS and MSc from São Paulo State University.

I will try to maintain this page as updated as possible. However, you can use the following as an updated list of my publications and other projects.

GitHub, Twitter, ResearchGate, Google Scholar, and LinkedIn.
